How to use Tihamer's Thingy:

If you wanna start a new thingy/roleplay, put it in the big input below this text. Type what you want the thingy to be about, then (if you want), you can put a URL of an image you want to appear next to the thingy heading, when you're done press enter or "Start".
If you want to reply to a thingy, scroll to the bottom of it and type in the thing that says "Reply to thingy here", the when you're done, press enter or "Submit replying to thingy".
You may edit your messages by clicking on the "Change" button below to them (it appears when you hover over your message), (you cannot edit them again once you've refreshed the page or a mod has changed them). Once your done editing, press enter, just leaving the edited message doesn't change it. You may delete messages by editing them then leaving nothing in the input, this will make your message turn into "[DELETED]". (Only people who have powers/hosts can change headings.)
There are settings listed below, you may change them to your liking.
If you would like to copy a thingy, you may click "Copy thingy" below the heading. It will copy it to your settings, (as in, if you're not showing names, it won't copy those, and it'll round your seperation in lines to see how many lines go between each paragraph).
If you would like to make a thingy smaller, you may push "Collapse thingy to tiny thingy" below the heading. (This will also get rid of the image if there is one.) If something new gets posted in the collapsed thingy, the heading will turn a dark blue color.
Only the dev (me/Tihamer) and the teacher (Lesley) know how to get the code and edit other people's messages.
